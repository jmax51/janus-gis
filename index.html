Janus France & WPI Impact Evaluation and Community Outreach

This project evaluates the community impact of Janus France, a community bike workshop association based in Lyon, France. Using a mixed-methods approach combining surveys, interviews, and GIS mapping, we assess how Janus France contributes to sustainable urban mobility and community well-being.
